ERD - Entity Relationship Diagram

Shows entities, their attributes, and relationships using crow's foot notation. Each relationship displays cardinality: one (●) to many (crow's foot ⟨).

Crow's Foot Notation Legend

○──── One (exactly one) - mandatory relationship
○────⟨ One-to-Many - crow's foot indicates "many" side
(PK) Primary Key - uniquely identifies each record
(FK) Foreign Key - references primary key in another table
1 : M Cardinality notation (One-to-Many relationship)
