EERD - Enhanced Entity Relationship Diagram

Highlights the associative entity (PolicyPurchase) that resolves the many-to-many relationship between Customer and Policy. This bridge entity contains additional attributes about the purchase transaction.

EERD Notation Legend

⬥ Red Border ⬥ Associative Entity - resolves many-to-many relationships
M : N Many-to-Many relationship (needs resolution)
1 : M One-to-Many relationship (normal relationship)
(PK) + (FK) Composite key from parent entities becomes part of associative entity

Key Concept: Associative Entity

PolicyPurchase is an associative (bridge/junction) entity that:

  • Resolves the M:N relationship between Customer and Policy
  • Contains foreign keys from both parent entities (CustomerID, PolicyID)
  • Adds its own meaningful attributes (PurchaseDate, StartDate, EndDate, Status)
  • Has its own primary key (PurchaseID)
  • Represents a real-world concept: the act of purchasing a policy
